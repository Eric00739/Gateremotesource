FAQ

What is the MOQ?

Standard MOQ is 100 pcs per model. Custom logo printing is also available from 100 pcs.

Are batteries included?

Yes. All remotes come with high-quality pre-installed batteries, ready for installers to use right out of box.

What is the typical lead time?

For regular models, lead time is usually 15–20 days after order confirmation. For large or custom OEM orders, our team will confirm the schedule with you.

Can you print our logo or use custom packaging?

Yes. We support custom logo printing and custom packaging from 100 pcs per model. Send us your logo file and we'll prepare a mockup for approval.

What warranty do you offer?

We provide a 2-year warranty on all remotes and receivers. Any quality issues will be replaced in your next shipment.
