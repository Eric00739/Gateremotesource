Shipping & Delivery Information

Worldwide shipping for wholesale garage and gate remote replacements

Shipping Methods

Express Air Freight

  • DHL Express: 3-5 business days worldwide
  • FedEx International: 3-7 business days worldwide
  • UPS Worldwide: 4-8 business days worldwide

Tracking available for all shipments

Sea Freight

  • 20ft Container: 25-35 days to major ports
  • 40ft Container: 30-45 days to major ports
  • LCL (Less than Container Load): 20-30 days

Economical for large orders over 500pcs

Local Delivery

  • Dongguan Area: 1-2 business days
  • Guangzhou/Shenzhen: 1-3 business days
  • Shanghai: 1-2 business days

Available for sample orders and small quantities

Shipping Costs

Cost Factors

  • Weight & Volume: Calculated based on total shipment weight and dimensions
  • Destination: Varies by country and shipping method
  • Fuel Surcharges: May apply based on current fuel prices
  • Customs & Duties: Buyer responsibility, varies by destination country

Cost Estimates

Small Orders (100-500pcs): Express air freight typically $50-150

Medium Orders (500-2000pcs): Express air freight typically $30-80 per kg

Large Orders (2000+pcs): Sea freight typically $5-20 per kg

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive tracking information via email:

Express Shipments

  • Tracking number within 24 hours of shipment
  • Online tracking with real-time updates
  • Delivery confirmation email upon arrival

Sea Freight Shipments

  • Bill of Lading (B/L) provided
  • Container tracking number
  • Port of arrival and estimated delivery date
  • Customs broker contact information

Special Requirements

Battery Shipping

Battery shipments require special handling due to transportation regulations:

  • UN38.3 compliant packaging required
  • Separate battery documentation
  • Additional processing time may apply

Customs Documentation

For international orders, please provide:

  • Commercial invoice with HS codes
  • Packing list with detailed descriptions
  • Certificate of Origin (CO)
  • Insurance certificate (if required)
