Information We Collect

When you visit our website or contact us for wholesale garage and gate remote replacements, we may collect certain information to provide you with our products and services.

Personal Information We Collect

  • Name and Contact Details: When you fill out our contact forms or send us emails
  • Company Information: Your company name, role, and business details
  • Communication Preferences: How you prefer to be contacted (email, phone, WhatsApp)
  • Technical Requirements: Specific product needs, quantities, and customization requests
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and which pages you visit

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Products and Services: Process your orders, provide technical support, and respond to inquiries
  • To Improve Our Website: Analyze traffic patterns and user behavior to enhance user experience
  • To Communicate With You: Respond to inquiries, send product updates, and provide customer service
  • For Marketing: Send information about new products, special offers, and industry insights (with your consent)

Information Sharing

We may share your information with trusted third parties for the following purposes:

Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: To process wholesale orders and payments securely
  • Shipping Partners: DHL, FedEx, and other logistics providers for order delivery
  • Form Processing: Formspree for secure form submissions and lead management

Legal Requirements

  • Government Authorities: When required by law for customs, export, or regulatory compliance
  • Business Partners: With your explicit consent for specific collaborations or partnerships
  • Legal Protection: To protect our rights, property, and business interests

Data Protection

Security Measures

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmissions use secure HTTPS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information only for authorized personnel
  • Secure Storage: Your information is stored on secure servers with regular backups
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the information necessary to provide our services

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information at any time
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)

  • Complaint: File a complaint with data protection authorities if you believe your rights have been violated

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website:

What We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security

  • Marketing Cookies: To remember your preferences and show relevant advertisements
  • Session Cookies: To maintain your login state during your visit

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

International Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Data Transfer Basis
